Who Are We?

Life is the one and only personal trainer marketplace, where you can discover, connect, and be guided by your favorite certified trainers and influencers.

We carefully onboard trainers for their reliability and verified qualifications, so you can focus on achieving the best results in your health and fitness journey.

User Features 👤

  • Trainer Marketplace 🌍 — The one and only place to find, connect, and train with certified personal trainers and influencers.
  • Ad-Free Experience 🚫📢 — No annoying ads, just a clean and focused fitness journey.
  • Verified Trainers ✅ — Every trainer is approved based on qualifications and influence.
  • Smart Trainer Search 🔍 — Filter by region, gender, discounts, and ratings to find your perfect match.
  • Flexible Subscriptions 💳 — Choose the bundle that fits your lifestyle and budget.
  • Community Ratings ⭐ — Rate your trainer and help others make informed choices.
  • Trainer-Guided Plans 📅 — Get personalized workouts and daily meal plans from your trainer.
  • Meal Flexibility 🍽️ — Swap in your own meals while staying on track with your plan.
  • Progress Tracking 📈 — Trainers monitor your workouts and meals daily to keep you accountable.
  • Safe & Secure Payments 🔒 — We never store card details. Payments are handled through trusted gateways.
  • All-in-One Trackers 📝 — Meals, workouts, body measurements — all synced in one place.
  • Fitness Calculators 📊 — Body Fat, TDEE, BMI, BMR, and Macros with full app integration.
  • Simple & Intuitive UI 🎨 — A clean, user-friendly design that makes your journey easy and enjoyable.
  • Email Notifications 📧 — Stay updated on all important details directly in your inbox.

Trainer Features 🧑‍🏫

  • Secure Content 🔐 — Upload your videos without worrying about leaks or unauthorized sharing.
  • Predefined Plans 📋 — Create meal and workout templates to quickly assign to trainees.
  • Custom Coaching ✍️ — Modify or create unique plans tailored to each trainee’s needs.
  • Progress Monitoring 📊 — Track workouts, meals, and body measurements throughout subscriptions.
  • Onboarding Questionnaires 📝 — Collect trainee preferences, medical info, and goals before they subscribe.
  • Subscription Tracking ⏱️ — Easily see start, end, and bundle details for each trainee.
  • Regional Pricing 🌎 — Offer custom pricing per country or currency (EGP, USD, etc.).
  • Profile Customization 🖊️ — Update certifications, capacity, questionnaires, and social media links.
  • Wallet & Payouts 💰 — Manage invoices easily; payouts are biweekly and transparent.
  • Zero Subscription Fees 🚀 — Free to use! Only minimal transfer and gateway fees apply. We will notify you through email for any fees changes.
  • Easy Transition 🔄 — Bring your existing trainees onboard for free (first 5 days of signup).
  • Automated Notifications 📧 — Get email updates for subscriptions, payments, and workflows.
