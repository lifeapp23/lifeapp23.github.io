Who Are We?

Life is the one and only personal trainer marketplace, where you can discover, connect, and be guided by your favorite certified trainers and influencers.

We carefully onboard trainers for their reliability and verified qualifications, so you can focus on achieving the best results in your health and fitness journey.

Features 🚀

  • Trainer Marketplace 🧑‍🏫 — Search and connect with trainers by price, rating, country, gender, and qualifications. Trainers can assign and track your meals and workouts daily for guided progress.
  • Meal Tracker 🍽️ — Track your nutrition with 1,500+ predefined meals or add custom meals.
  • Workout Tracker 🏋️ — Access 500+ exercises or add custom workouts tailored to your goals.
  • Body Measurement Tracker 📏 — Log your measurements and plan target goals with specific timelines.
  • Smart Calculators 📊 — Body Fat %, BMR, BMI, TDEE, and Macro calculators with flexible options (activity levels, diet types, goals).

Coming Soon 🌟

  • Community 🫂 — Share, engage, and connect with posts from your favorite trainers and influencers.
